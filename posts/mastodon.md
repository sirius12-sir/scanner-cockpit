Built the minimal end of #localfirst: a single-file, no-backend, no-sync "cockpit" for people running many unrelated domains at once.

It's organized around "where was I?" per domain, not the todo list — so re-entry cost is ~0. One HTML file, your data is a plain JSON on your own disk. Opens in 10 years without a toolchain.

Less looking for users than for the rare person who works this way — depth in many domains, held by structure not willpower.
